Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Countdown Timer ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our App, you may provide us with the following information:

  • Event Information: Event names, dates, times, and descriptions you create for your countdowns
  • Custom Designs: Background images and customization preferences you select
  • User Preferences: App settings and display preferences

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We do not automatically collect any personal information, device information, or usage data. The App functions entirely offline and does not transmit data to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

All information you provide is stored locally on your device and is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To display your countdown timers and events
  • To save your customization preferences and designs
  • To provide the core functionality of the App

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not:

  • Store your data on remote servers
  • Transmit your data over the internet
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Back up your data to cloud services

4. Third-Party Services

Our App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data remains entirely on your device.

5. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for countdown backgrounds (optional)
  • Photo Library/Gallery: To select images from your device for countdown backgrounds (optional)
  • Notifications: To send you reminders about your countdowns (optional)

All permissions are optional and only used for the specific features you choose to enable. We do not access these permissions without your explicit consent.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device and we do not collect or transmit any data, the App can be safely used by children under parental guidance.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data stored locally on your device. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We recommend:

  • Using device security features (passcode, biometric authentication)
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Being cautious about granting physical access to your device

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete individual countdowns within the App
  • Clear the App's data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the App from your device

Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can access all your data within the App at any time
  • Modify: You can edit or update your countdowns and preferences
  • Delete: You can delete individual items or all data through the App or device settings
  • Export: You can take screenshots or backup your device data using standard device backup methods

10. International Data Transfers

We do not transfer any data internationally or to any servers, as all data remains on your local device.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Providing an in-app notification (if applicable)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

13. Consent

By using our App, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
